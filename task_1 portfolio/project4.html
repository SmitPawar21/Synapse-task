Weather Website

A web application designed to provide users with current and forecasted weather information for any location. It aims to deliver accurate and up-to-date weather data in an intuitive and visually appealing format. Users can check the weather for their current location or search for weather in other cities globally.

Objectives

  • Real-Time Weather Information: Display current weather conditions and forecasts.

  • User-Friendly Interface: Offer an easy-to-navigate, visually engaging interface.

  • Responsiveness: Ensure the website is functional and visually appealing on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

  • Geolocation and Search: Allow users to get weather information based on their current location or search for other locations.
